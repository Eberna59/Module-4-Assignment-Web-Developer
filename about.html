Our History

The "Grey Day" tour is the annual music festival-style concert tour put on by the New Orleans rap duo $uicideboy$. First held in 2019, the tour has grown into a major international summer event that unites rap and hardcore music fans

Our Mission

The $uicideboy$ mission is to save "100,000 souls," which they are pursuing through their music, sobriety, mental health advocacy, and partnerships, aiming to connect with and support fans who resonate with their shared experiences of struggle, addiction, and recovery.

Our Team

$uicideboy$, Germ, Chetta, Night Lovell and Shakewell
